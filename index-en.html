About me

My name is Stefan Ortlieb. I am a psychologist, lecturer in psychology and systemic counselor in training (DGSF). As a psychological counselor, I support individuals, couples, families and teams in their search for resources, balance, new perspectives and themselves. My areas of focus include work-life balance and coping with crises during studies, doctoral research and professional life.

Qualifications

  • Diploma in Psychology (University of Bamberg)
  • PhD in Psychology (University of Bamberg)
  • Systemic Counselor in training (DGFS)

Basic Approach

🔄

Systemic

Our work is based on a systemic understanding of counseling and therapy. We always view people in the context of their relationships, life histories and current life situations.

💪

Resource-Oriented

We believe in an appreciative and resource-oriented approach. We assume that every person brings many skills and experiences with them, which sometimes fade from view during difficult times.

🎯

Solution-Focused

In our work together, we want to create a space where new perspectives can emerge and your own options for action become clear again.

🏺

At Eye Level

In a protected setting, I meet you with respect, empathy and genuine care.

Offers

I offer counseling in German and English. Sessions can take place in person or online, indoors - in our practice or in a museum - or outside, sitting or walking:
How about trying a dialogue walk, a small "soul journey" or a relaxed running session (my limit is 10 kilometers ;)?

Classic: Systemic Counseling in a Protected Space

In a protected environment, we work on your topics. Whether you're facing personal crises, relationship questions or professional transitions – I support you with appreciation and a resource-oriented approach on your way to new perspectives.

Dynamic: Running Analysis

Running together, we use movement as a path to new perspectives. The rhythm of running creates a natural space for deep conversations. Practical, clear and solution-focused – no meditation cushion needed.

Aesthetic Experience: Therapy and Museum Visit

Art and culture as a path to self-reflection. Together we explore in the museum how artworks can open new perspectives and illuminate your personal topics. A unique space for deep conversation processes.

The practice
