About me

As a systemic counselor, I support people on their way to greater clarity and new perspectives. With many years of experience and a holistic view, I help you discover your resources and develop effective solutions.

Together, in a trusting setting, we create space for change and personal growth.

Qualifications

  • PhD Psychologist (University)
  • Systemic Counselor (in training)

My therapeutic approach

Systemic counseling views people within their full relationship system. Challenges are not seen in isolation, but in the context of family, work, and social environment.

🔄

Holistic perspective

Together, we look at all areas of life and their interactions to find sustainable solutions.

🏃

Running Analysis

While running together, we use movement as a way to access new perspectives in a clear, practical, and solution-oriented process.

🎯

Solution-focused

Instead of analyzing problems, we focus on concrete steps toward your goals.

🤝

Respectful

In a protected space, I meet you with respect, empathy, and genuine care.
